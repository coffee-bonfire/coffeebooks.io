Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 8, 2024

[CoffeeBookApp] (hereinafter referred to as "this App") respects the privacy of users and strives to protect personal information. This Privacy Policy sets forth the handling of personal information in this App.

1. Handling of Personal Information

This App does not collect users' personal information. Image data and other information registered in the picture book are all stored on the user's terminal and are not sent to an external server.

2. Access to Camera and Photos

This App may request permission to access the camera and photos in order to take or select image data to register in the picture book. These permissions are only valid if the user explicitly grants them, and if they are not granted, the App will not be able to access the camera and photos. The acquired images are stored on the terminal and are not sent externally.

3. Network Connection

This App requires an Internet connection to display and update picture book data. However, the user's personal information and image data on the terminal are not sent externally.

4. Data Analysis

This App may conduct anonymized data analysis to improve the quality of the App and to investigate the cause of crashes. The information collected does not identify individual users.

5. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may change the contents of this Privacy Policy without notice. The revised Privacy Policy will be posted within this App.
